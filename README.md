# Optimizing-Power-Allocation-During-Overload  
Abstract:  
In urban areas, ensuring stable and uninterrupted power supply is crucial for maintaining critical
services and sustaining daily operations. However, during overload situations, where demand
exceeds available supply, effective power allocation becomes paramount. This paper proposes a
priority-based algorithm for power allocation during overload scenarios, aiming to prioritize
critical zones while optimizing resource utilization. The algorithm considers factors such as zone
classification, power requirements, real-time monitoring, emergency protocols, and
communication channels for coordinated action. Through dynamic adjustment and continuous
evaluation, the algorithm seeks to enhance the resilience of urban power grids and mitigate the
impact of disruptions.

Authors: Kaushiki Wankhade, Harshvardhan Pawar, Gajanan Barve.
